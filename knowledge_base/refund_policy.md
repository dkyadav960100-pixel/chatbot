# Refund and Return Policy

## Overview

At TechCorp, we stand behind our products and services. This policy outlines our commitment to customer satisfaction through our refund and return procedures.

## Software and Subscription Services

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

All new subscriptions come with a 30-day money-back guarantee:
- Request a refund within 30 days of initial purchase
- Full refund, no questions asked
- Applies to first-time subscribers only
- Does not apply to renewals or upgrades

### Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time:
- Cancel before your next billing date to avoid charges
- Access continues until the end of your current billing period
- No partial refunds for unused time in monthly plans
- Annual plans: Prorated refund available within first 90 days

### How to Cancel

1. Log into your TechCorp account
2. Navigate to Settings > Subscription
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Complete the cancellation survey (optional)
5. Receive confirmation email within 24 hours

### Downgrade Policy

You can downgrade your plan at any time:
- Downgrade takes effect at next billing cycle
- No refunds for the difference in current cycle
- All features above your new plan will be disabled
- Data exceeding new plan limits will be preserved for 30 days

## Hardware Products

### Return Window

Hardware products can be returned within 14 days:
- Products must be in original packaging
- All accessories must be included
- Products must be in resalable condition
- Original receipt or order confirmation required

### Return Process

1. Contact support to initiate a return
2. Receive Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
3. Package item securely with RMA number visible
4. Ship to provided return address
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days of receipt

### Exceptions

The following items cannot be returned:
- Opened software packages
- Customized or personalized items
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Products damaged by user misuse
- Items purchased more than 14 days ago

### Defective Products

For defective products:
- Report within 30 days for free replacement
- Extended warranty coverage varies by product
- Contact support with photos/videos of defect
- We cover return shipping for defective items

## Enterprise Contracts

Enterprise customers with custom contracts should refer to their specific agreement terms. Generally:

- Custom refund terms as per contract
- Account manager assistance for all billing inquiries
- Quarterly billing adjustments available
- Early termination fees may apply

## Processing Times

### Refund Processing
- Credit card refunds: 5-10 business days
- Bank transfer refunds: 10-15 business days
- PayPal refunds: 3-5 business days
- Store credit: Immediate

### Factors Affecting Processing
- Bank processing times vary
- International transactions may take longer
- Holidays and weekends may cause delays
- Large refunds may require additional verification

## Billing Disputes

If you notice an incorrect charge:

1. **Review Your Invoice**: Check your invoice in Settings > Billing
2. **Contact Support**: Email billing@techcorp.example.com
3. **Provide Details**: Include invoice number and discrepancy
4. **Resolution**: We respond within 2 business days

### Chargebacks

Before filing a chargeback with your bank:
- Contact us first to resolve the issue
- Chargebacks incur a $25 processing fee
- Repeated chargebacks may result in account termination
- We provide documentation for legitimate disputes

## Special Circumstances

### Account Compromise
If your account was charged due to unauthorized access:
- Report immediately to security@techcorp.example.com
- We will investigate within 24 hours
- Fraudulent charges will be fully refunded
- We may require identity verification

### Service Outages
For prolonged service disruptions:
- Check status.techcorp.example.com for updates
- Service credits automatically applied for SLA breaches
- Enterprise customers: Per-contract compensation
- No refunds for scheduled maintenance

### Feature Changes
If we remove a feature you rely on:
- 90-day notice provided for major changes
- Transition assistance available
- Prorated refund if feature was primary use case
- Alternative solutions suggested where possible
