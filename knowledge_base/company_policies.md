# Company Policies - TechCorp Inc.

## Introduction

Welcome to TechCorp Inc.! This document outlines our key company policies that all employees should be familiar with.

## Working Hours

- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
- Flexible work arrangements are available with manager approval.
- Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week.
- Core hours (when everyone should be available) are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- All full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance for periods longer than 3 days.
- Unused leave can be carried over up to 5 days to the next year.

### Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
- A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.
- Sick leave cannot be carried over to the next year.

### Parental Leave
- Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of paid parental leave.
- Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks of paid parental leave.
- Leave can be taken flexibly within the first year of the child's birth or adoption.

## Expense Reimbursement

### Travel Expenses
- Economy class flights for trips under 6 hours.
- Business class permitted for flights over 6 hours with manager approval.
- Hotel accommodations up to $200/night in standard cities, $300/night in premium cities.
- Per diem of $75 for domestic travel, $100 for international travel.

### Equipment and Supplies
- All work-related equipment purchases must be pre-approved.
- Equipment over $500 requires department head approval.
- Personal devices used for work may qualify for a monthly stipend of $50.

## Code of Conduct

1. **Integrity**: Act honestly and ethically in all business dealings.
2. **Respect**: Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with dignity and respect.
3. **Confidentiality**: Protect company and client confidential information.
4. **Compliance**: Follow all applicable laws and company policies.
5. **Safety**: Maintain a safe working environment for everyone.

## Grievance Procedure

If you have a concern or complaint:
1. First, try to resolve the issue directly with the person involved.
2. If unresolved, speak with your immediate manager.
3. If still unresolved, contact HR for formal mediation.
4. Final appeals can be made to the Ethics Committee.

All grievances are treated confidentially and without retaliation.
